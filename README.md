# YuSleep
---
Have you ever met a situation, when you want to fall asleep while on youtube it's playing some asmr video and you want it to play whole night and morning, but auto-play isn't working?
I met, so i decided to create simple js fastapi site, where you can load some asmr(and not only them) videos and it will play it in loop until your phone ruin.
---
Before using, make sure you setted brightness on 1% to save your phone battery :)
